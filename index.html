Kakkamvelly Sreekrishna Temple

A Sacred Abode of Lord Krishna

Kakkamvelly Sreekrishna Temple is a revered Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Sreekrishna, situated in the Kozhikode district of Kerala. The temple stands as a spiritual beacon for devotees from across the region, embodying the rich cultural and religious heritage of Kerala's temple tradition.

The presiding deity, Sreekrishna – the eighth avatar of Lord Vishnu – is worshipped here with deep devotion. The temple follows the traditional Tantric rituals prescribed in the Tanthrasamuchayam, the canonical text governing Kerala temple worship.

Surrounded by the lush greenery of Kerala's Malabar region, the temple complex is a place of peace, devotion, and cultural continuity. Regular poojas, festivals, and community gatherings strengthen the bond between the temple and the local community.

Principal Deity: Sreekrishna
District: Kozhikode, Kerala
Tradition: Kerala Tantric Ritual
Open to All Devotees

Festivals & Special Events

✦
🎉

Ashtami Rohini
(Krishna Jayanti)

The most important festival celebrating the birth of Lord Krishna. Elaborate decorations, special poojas, and devotional programmes mark this auspicious day.

Chingam (Aug–Sep)
🌸

Vishu

The Kerala New Year celebrated with Vishukkani (auspicious first sight), special poojas, and traditional customs marking the beginning of the Malayalam new year.

Medam (Apr–May)
🌺

Onam

Kerala's harvest festival celebrated with Pookalam (floral decorations), Onasadhya (traditional feast), and special prayers for prosperity and well-being.

Chingam (Aug–Sep)
⭐

Thiruvathira

Observed by women with special rituals, Kaikottikali (clap dance), and prayers for the well-being of their husbands and families.

Dhanu (Dec–Jan)
🪔

Ekadasi

The eleventh day of each lunar fortnight observed as a fasting day with special Vishnu poojas and devotional discourses (pravachanam).

Monthly
🌙

Guruvayur Ekadasi

Special celebrations on the Ekadasi falling in the Malayalam month of Vrishchikam, one of the most significant days in Vaishnavite temples across Kerala.

Vrishchikam (Nov–Dec)

How to Reach

Address

Kakkamvelly Sreekrishna Temple,
Kozhikode District, Kerala – 673xxx

By Air

Nearest airport: Calicut International Airport (CCJ), Karipur – approx. 30–40 km from Kozhikode city.

By Train

Kozhikode (Calicut) Railway Station is well-connected to major cities across India. Local buses and auto-rickshaws are available from the station.

By Bus

KSRTC and private buses connect Kozhikode to all major towns in Kerala. Local autos and cabs are available for the final stretch to the temple.
